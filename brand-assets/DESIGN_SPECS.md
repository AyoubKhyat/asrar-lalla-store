## Print Specifications

### Label Printing
- **Resolution:** 300dpi minimum, vector preferred
- **Color space:** CMYK for offset, RGB for digital
- **Bleed:** 2mm on all sides
- **Stock:** White matte waterproof vinyl (for wet/bathroom environments)
- **Finish:** Soft-touch matte laminate + spot UV on logo flower and brand name
- **Adhesive:** Permanent, waterproof

### Card Printing
- **Stock:** 350gsm silk art card
- **Finish:** Soft-touch matte lamination both sides
- **Special:** Spot UV varnish on logo and "Merci" text
- **Cut:** Die-cut with 3mm rounded corners

### Box Printing
- **Material:** E-flute corrugated cardboard (1.5mm)
- **Exterior:** Full-color CMYK print on white liner
- **Interior:** 1-color (pink #FFCFE1) flood coat
- **Finish:** Matte varnish exterior
- **Closure:** Tuck-in flap (phase 1), magnetic closure (phase 2)

## Typography Specifications (Print)

### Logo Wordmark
- **Font:** Outfit Bold 700
- **Size:** Proportional to icon (1.5× icon height)
- **Tracking:** 0.15em (wide)
- **Case:** ALL CAPS

### Product Names (Labels)
- **Font:** Outfit ExtraBold 800
- **Size:** 18-22pt (front label), 11pt (back label)
- **Tracking:** 0.05em
- **Case:** ALL CAPS

### Body Text (Labels/Cards)
- **Font:** DM Sans Regular 400
- **Size:** 7-9pt
- **Leading:** 1.6×
- **Color:** #6B6B6B

### Arabic Text
- **Font:** Amiri Regular 400
- **Size:** 80% of corresponding French text
- **Color:** #9CA3AF (muted)

## Instagram Grid Layout System

### 9-Post Launch Grid (first 9 posts)
```
[ Product flat lay ] [ Brand quote    ] [ Product close-up ]
[ Before/After     ] [ LOGO (center)  ] [ Texture shot     ]
[ Customer review  ] [ Routine steps  ] [ Product in use   ]
```

### Ongoing Grid Pattern (repeating 3-row cycle)
```
Row 1: Product | Lifestyle | Product
Row 2: Quote/Tip | Review | Behind scenes
Row 3: Carousel | Reel cover | Product
```

### Color Balance Rule
- 60% white/light backgrounds
- 25% pink accents
- 15% lifestyle/natural tones

## Instagram Feed Template Types (12)

1. **Product Showcase** — Single product, pink bg, name + price
2. **Before/After** — Split screen, left gray / right pink
3. **Customer Review** — White card, pink stars, quote marks
4. **Price Reveal** — Product + large price, crossed old price
5. **Routine Steps** — 4-panel grid, numbered steps
6. **Quote/Tip** — Pink gradient bg, white text, brand pill
7. **Pack Promo** — Multiple products, savings callout
8. **Ingredient Spotlight** — Single ingredient, facts
9. **Flash Sale** — Bold countdown style, urgency colors
10. **New Arrival** — "NOUVEAU" badge, reveal style
11. **Flat Lay** — Multiple products arranged aesthetically
12. **Milestone** — Number celebration, confetti elements

## Instagram Story Template Types (12)

1. **Product Promo** — Full product with CTA
2. **Poll** — "Which product?" two-option layout
3. **Quiz** — Beauty question with answer reveal
4. **Countdown** — Sale/launch countdown
5. **Review Highlight** — WhatsApp screenshot style
6. **Swipe Up/Link** — Product + arrow CTA
7. **Behind the Scenes** — Raw photo + brand frame
8. **Order Prep** — Packaging video frame
9. **Price List** — Multiple products with prices

12. **Thank You** — Post-purchase appreciation

## TikTok Cover Template Types (20)

1-5: Product hook covers (bold text + product)
6-10: Before/After covers (split frame)
11-14: Tutorial covers (step numbers)
15-17: Humor/relatable covers (emoji + text)
18-20: Social proof covers (stars + review quote)

All covers follow the same template structure:
- Top: brand pill (white on transparent)
- Center: photo/video thumbnail area
- Bottom 1/3: gradient overlay + hook text (Outfit 800, white, 48-56px)
- Bottom bar: CTA pill (pink rounded)
